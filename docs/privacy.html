Introduction

Touch & Pick ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Touch & Pick is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information for the purpose of serving personalized ads. This may include:

For more information about how Google processes your data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Data Usage

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data. The only data handled by our app is processed by AdMob for advertising purposes, as described above.

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Turn off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
