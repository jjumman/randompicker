Touch & Pick

The Fun Random Selection Game

About

Touch & Pick is the easiest and most exciting way to pick random winners with your friends! Perfect for games, decisions, and choosing winners.

How to Play

  1. Touch the screen with multiple fingers (2-10 players)
  2. Wait 1 second for the game to start
  3. Watch the exciting spin animation (1.5 seconds)
  4. One winner will be selected randomly!

Features

  • Beautiful, colorful touch animations
  • Fast and smooth selection (only 2.5 seconds total!)
  • Perfect for 2-10 players
  • 100% fair and random selection
  • iPhone optimized
  • Satisfying haptic feedback
  • No login required

Perfect For

  • Choosing who goes first in games
  • Making group decisions
  • Party games and ice breakers
  • Classroom activities
  • Settling friendly debates
  • Random team selection
